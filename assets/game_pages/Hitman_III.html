Game Description

HITMAN 3 is the stealth game developed and published by IO Interactive. As the eighth main installment in the HITMAN series, it concludes the World of Assassination trilogy. You follow the genetically engineered assassin Agent 47 on a mission to hunt down the leaders of the secretive organization Providence.

Diverse Locations & Sandbox Gameplay

HITMAN 3 features six new diverse locations: Dubai, Dartmoor, Berlin, Chongqing, Mendoza, and the Carpathian Mountains. A new island location set in the Andaman Sea was introduced post-launch. Each mission offers large sandbox locations for free exploration with various assassination opportunities and methods, enhanced story-driven missions, and persistent shortcuts for multiple playthroughs.

Key Characters

Agent 47, the central character, is a genetically engineered assassin trained from childhood. Other notable characters include Diana Burnwood (Agent 47's handler), Lucas Grey (a rogue mercenary and ally), Arthur Edwards (the Constant), and The Partners: Carl Ingram, Marcus Stuyvesant, and Alexa Carlisle, who are the main targets in the game.

Game Features

The game includes instinct vision to highlight relevant items, a wide range of disguises and stealth tactics, ability to import maps and progress from HITMAN 1 and 2, PC and PlayStation VR compatibility for a first-person experience, multiple game modes including Escalation Contracts, Elusive Targets, Contracts Mode, and the new Freelancer Mode.

Missions and Locations

  • Dubai: Set in the world's tallest skyscraper, the Scepter
  • Dartmoor: A murder-mystery mission in an English manor
  • Berlin: A night club setting with multiple targets
  • Chongqing: A high-tech city with a data storage facility
  • Mendoza: A vineyard with complex mission parameters
  • Carpathian Mountains: A thrilling finale set on a moving train
